Tiffiniy Cheng

Tiffiniy is our co-founder and co-director. She started Open Congress, Miro, almost got the SAFE Act passed until Chris Dodd pulled a late night vote - her work is on art, culture and structural power issues.

Holmes Wilson

Holmes is a co-founder of Fight for the Future. He also co-founded Miro, Open Congress, and Amara, and ran online campaigns for the FSF.

Chuck Harmston

Chuck helps define and develop our projects and campaigns. He was previously a technical lead at Threespot, working with clients such as the Peace Corps, Robert Wood Johnson Foundation, Knight Foundation, and ACLU.

Ali Ali

Joshua Blount

Joshua (@stickwithjosh to his friends) is our developer, hacking away at our various websites and projects. He cares deeply about carbonated beverages, the internet and you, and he previously worked at Canonical on Ubuntu One.

Douglas Schatz

Douglas graduated from Hampshire College in 2011 after writing a senior thesis entitled, "Sampling Copyright: an examination of, and proposed solution to, the fight between digital sampling and American copyright law". He has been interning at Fight for the Future ever since.
